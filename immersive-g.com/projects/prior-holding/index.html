Blending Cultures Digitally for Prior Holdings’ Hospitality-Centric Website

Launch website
An immersive website balancing Japanese heritage with Mediterranean elegance and adaptability.
We designed a dynamic website for Prior Holdings, featuring a ribbon motif, seamless animations, and dual‑language typography to reflect their cultural influences and hospitality ethos.

  • Strategy & Concept
  • UX/UI
  • Art direction
  • Motion design, 3D
  • Development
  • Sound design
Exploring Prior Holdings’ world through modern, immersive digital design.
The ribbon symbolized elegance and adaptability, enhanced by organic textures and smooth interactions crafted with Vue.js and WebGL, offering a visually engaging experience.
Dual‑language support integrated Kanji and Western typography, ensuring an equally immersive journey for Japanese and English audiences while showcasing Prior Holdings’ dedication to cultural harmony.
This project highlighted our ability to merge cultural storytelling and modern design, delivering an immersive digital platform that reflects Prior Holdings’ leadership and vision in hospitality.
