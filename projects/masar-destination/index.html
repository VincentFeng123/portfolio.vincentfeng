Creating a Digital Journey for Masar’s Visionary Destination

Launch website
An interactive experience highlighting Masar’s transformative role in Mecca’s urban future.
We modeled Masar’s 1.2 million square meters in WebGL, crafting a fast‑loading, day-and-night interactive platform showcasing its key urban components and visionary offerings.

  • Strategy & Concept
  • UX/UI
  • Art Direction
  • Motion Design, 3D
  • Development
  • Sound design
Exploring Masar’s innovative urban landscape through immersive digital storytelling.
The platform allowed users to toggle between day and night views, blending virtual components with reality to showcase Masar’s dynamic and transformative urban environment.
We developed a 45‑second video for events and designed Masar’s main website, aligning with the brand’s vision and demonstrating our ability to deliver across formats and platforms.
This project reflected Masar’s mission to enhance urban life, using cutting‑edge technology to create a seamless, immersive experience that positions it as a landmark destination in the heart of Mecca.
