Crafting Hatom’s Immersive Digital Universe with Symbolic Storytelling

Launch website
An artistic digital experience showcasing Hatom’s crypto platform and technological evolution.
We created an immersive platform for Hatom, centered around symbolic storytelling through a griffon’s evolution, illustrating their ecosystem expansion and leadership in cryptocurrency innovation.

  • Strategy & Concept
  • UX/UI
  • Art Direction
  • Motion Design, 3D
  • Development
  • Sound design
Exploring Hatom’s journey through dynamic visuals and cutting‑edge interactivity.
The narrative begins with an egg hatching into a griffon, symbolizing Hatom’s growth and evolution, leading to a connected ecosystem of planets representing their expanding crypto protocols.
With vibrant visuals, voiceover‑driven discovery, and seamless navigation, the site balances artistic abstraction and technical depth, engaging users with Hatom’s innovative solutions and encouraging exploration.
This project elevated Hatom’s digital presence, combining symbolic storytelling, advanced interactivity, and immersive sound design to deliver a captivating platform that aligns perfectly with their brand and crypto expertise.
