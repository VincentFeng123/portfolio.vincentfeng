Launching Louis Vuitton’s Web 3 Vision with an Immersive NFT Platform

Launch website
An innovative digital platform introducing Louis Vuitton’s luxury NFT journey in Web3.
We created a 3D experiential website for Louis Vuitton, blending photorealistic visuals, interactive design, and storytelling to showcase their debut NFT collection, available on OpenSea, in an immersive digital space.

  • Strategy &Concept
  • UX/UI
  • Art Direction
  • Motion Design, 3D
  • Development
  • Sound design
Exploring Louis Vuitton’s NFT universe through luxury design and Web 3 innovation.
The platform featured 3D scenes with a virtual LV trunk, dynamic effects, and "Our Itinerary," teasing the NFTs, joining a waitlist, and offering a unique multi‑layered NFT experience.
Through stunning visuals, glitch transitions, and artistic 3D animation, the site captured Louis Vuitton’s signature themes of dreams and travel, setting a high bar for luxury Web 3 experiences.
This project showcased Louis Vuitton’s groundbreaking venture into blockchain, delivering an artistic and interactive digital experience that aligned with their elevated standards and strategic entry into Web 3 innovation.
